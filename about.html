Ryan|October 29th, 1999|Trinidadian|ASD

I'm a Trinidadian hobbyist artist who just wants to make art of what he wants and all that stuff.

I made this site because quite frankly, unless it's a community oriented place (like a forum or a chatroom/discord server), I hate posting my art on social media, mainly algorithm/metrics focused networks like Twitter (or as Melon Husk likes to call it, X, yh good luck finding "X" videos lol) and Instagram. It feels like an esoteric pissing match against artists and other creatives who are (aspiring) professionals to see who can get the most internet points. I also wanted to have a place for my friends to easily access all my artwork, so I can easily link them when I get the chance. That's how I got here.

Also, like I said, I am a hobbyist. I would rather get job unrelated to it and make art when I can rather than make it everyday but be hit with expectations to make art only others want by their standards with money at risk. That doesn't sound fun, and I want art to be fun.
